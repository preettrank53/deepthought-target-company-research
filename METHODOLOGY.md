# Target Company Research Methodology
### Prepared for DeepThought — Business Analytics Internship Audit

---

## 1. Core Objective & Sourcing Strategy
The objective was to identify and verify 25 specialty manufacturers along the Ahmedabad-Vadodara industrial corridor that strictly align with DeepThought’s "Federer" profile: revenue between ₹50Cr and ₹500Cr, high product differentiation, system maturity, and professionalized/Gen-2 leadership structures.

To avoid the pitfalls of generic search engine results, a multi-layered sourcing pipeline was utilized:
* **Industrial Cluster Crawling:** Sourced deep regional data from the Gujarat Industrial Development Corporation (GIDC) directories, specifically targeting the heavy chemical and processing hubs of **Vatva Phase I-III, Naroda, and Padra (Vadodara)**.
* **SME Public Listings:** Queried historical data from the **NSE Emerge and BSE SME** platforms to pinpoint mid-caps (e.g., Paragon Fine, Kronox Lab Sciences) with completely verifiable revenue brackets and transparent shareholding profiles.
* **Regulatory Database Validation:** Cross-referenced targets against regional lists for **WHO-GMP compliance, GLP certification, and DSIR-recognized R&D units** to ensure the separation of commodity processors from high-moat specialty chemical synthesis entities.

---

## 2. Filtering Workflow & Guardrails
A strict binary gate architecture was enforced prior to assigning any qualitative scores:
1. **Gate E1 (Producer):** Companies were comprehensively audited to confirm physical manufacturing infrastructure. Pure trading houses, export-import brokers, and standard Contract Research Organizations (CROs running pure service billing models without physical factories) were instantly deleted.
2. **Gate E2 (Location Continuity):** Ensured the primary operational hubs, plants, or founder headquarters were rooted locally in Gujarat—not merely registered dummy addresses.
3. **The Revenue Ceiling:** Fast-scaling market leaders such as *Aether Industries* or *Tatva Chintan* were explicitly documented and auto-disqualified because their financials have expanded beyond the upper ₹500 Crore boundary.

---

## 3. Key Insights & Sector Trends
* **The Moat Disconnect:** A clear distinction was observed between legacy family units operating on outdated ISO standards (e.g., ISO 9001:2008) and modern, technical-first operations. Legacy businesses frequently score poorly on Systems Maturity (C7), indicating an immediate opportunity for DeepThought’s operational consulting systems.
* **The Succession Paradigm:** High-scoring companies (like *Prima Chemicals*) showcased an ideal combination of a veteran founder coupled with an internationally trained, technically specialized Gen-2 leadership layer eager to deploy modern process frameworks.
